How does it work?

  • Set Your Monitoring Preferences

    Specify the websites and appointment types you need monitoring for. Our system customizes alerts based on your selected preferences, ensuring you never miss an opportunity.

  • Receive Real-time Alerts

    Our service continuously monitors your specified sites for openings. The moment an appointment becomes available, we instantly notify you via email or SMS, giving you the edge to secure your spot.

  • Secure Your Appointment

    With our timely alerts, you can quickly book available appointments. Our platform ensures you're always first in line, maximizing your chances to secure essential bookings without the hassle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our Website Monitoring Service is a proactive tool designed to monitor your selected websites and alert you in real-time when appointments become available. This service ensures you have the earliest opportunity to secure an appointment slot that meets your needs.

You can set up alerts by registering on our website, adding the websites and types of appointments you wish to monitor, and choosing how you'd like to be notified (email, SMS, etc.). Our system will then monitor these sites 24/7 and alert you the moment an appointment becomes available.

Our service offers both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic monitoring and alerts, while our premium plan offers enhanced features such as faster alerts, multiple appointment types, and premium support to ensure you never miss an important appointment.
